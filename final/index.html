Recent News:

Next up is Barbara Robinson's beloved Christmas stroy, "The Best Christmas Pageant Ever". The play will run Friday, December 5 - Sunday, December 14, 2014.

In this hilarious Christmas classic, a couple struggling to put on a church Christmas pageant is faced with casting the Herdman kids--probably the most inventively awful kids in history. You won't believe the mayhem - and the fun - when the Herdmans collide with the Christmas story head on!

This delightful comedy is adapted from the best-selling young adult book, and has become a holiday staple for theatres across the United States. Features plenty of great roles for children and adults, a few favorite Christmas carols, and a lot of laughs!

  • Show times are Fridays at 7:00 p.m. and Saturdays and Sundays at 3:00 p.m.
  • Ticket prices are $7.00 for adults and $5.00 for ages 12 and under.

Note: As our auditorium is a distance from the ticket counter, those needing assistance can be dropped off at the southwest door on the west side of the building.
