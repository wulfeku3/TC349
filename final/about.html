About Mid Michigan Family Theatre

Beginning back in 2007, Mid Michigan Family Theatre has proudly been providing its services to the community for nearly seven years and over 50 productions..

We are a non-profit community theatre organization that serves the greater Lansing area and provides classes, workshops, acting opportunities, and performances for people of all ages. Actors from age 4 to 70+ participate in family oriented productions 5 to 6 times per year.

MMFT is located at: Schmidt Community Center / 5825 Wise Road / Lansing, MI 48911
