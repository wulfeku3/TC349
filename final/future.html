Future Productions:

The two upcoming productions planned for Mid Michigan Family Theatre's seventh season are Romeo and Juliet and Mr. Toad's Mad Adventures. Auditions will be held at the theatre on December 8th and 9th at 6:45pm.

Romeo and Juliet will be directed by SaDonna Croft and Nicole Clyne and run at the end of February. Mr. Toad will be directed by Bill Gordon and will run during the beginning of March. Both shows' rehearsals begin in January.

The Mid Michigan Family Theatre holds open auditions for the majority of its shows. They consist of cold readings from the scripts along with other activities chosen by the director. This may include a prepared monologue, improvisations, or something similar. Unless noted, auditions are for all ages. Please contact Bill Gordon at (517) 339-2145 with questions.
