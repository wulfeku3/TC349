Ways to Get Involved::

There are many ways to contribute to Mid Michigan Family Theatre, and your support is very much appreciated. First and foremost would be your attendance at our various productions. The casts and crews work hard on bringing you a quality production and your attendance means a great deal to all of them. Letting others know about our productions and events will help us grow.

There are also numerous volunteering opportunities to support the theatre, including concession sales, ushering, hair and makeup, green room supervising, light and sound operators, rehearsal and audition helpers, publicity, fundraising, and more.

Finally, a financial contribution would be greatly appreciated if you find you have the opportunity. All of your donations are tax deductible. MMFT is a non-proft 501(c)(3) organization.
